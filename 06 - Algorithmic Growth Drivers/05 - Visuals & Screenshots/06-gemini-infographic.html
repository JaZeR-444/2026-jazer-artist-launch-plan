The Algorithm Scorecard

Not all streams are created equal. Spotify weights specific user actions to determine if a track is "engaging" (promote it) or "boring" (bury it). Save Ratio is the dominant positive signal.

Priority Action

"Heart" It.

The single most effective optimization is driving the Save Ratio. Every Call-To-Action (CTA) must ask fans to "Heart" the track. This signals retention to the algorithm.

The Killer

<30s Skips

A skip before the 30-second mark is a negative vote. It tells the engine the song is irrelevant to the listener, actively hurting reach.
