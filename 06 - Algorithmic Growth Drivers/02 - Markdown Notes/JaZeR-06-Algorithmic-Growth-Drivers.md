# 06 - Algorithmic Growth Drivers: JaZeR

## 1. The Algorithm Scorecard

| Signal | Weight | Why it Matters | How to Optimize |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Save Ratio** | **Highest** | *Tells Spotify "This song is good."* | *Ask fans to "Heart" the track in every CTA.* |
| **Repeat Listens** | **High** | *Tells Spotify "This song is addictive."* | *Catchy hooks, interesting transitions.* |
| **Skip Rate (<30s)** | **Negative** | *Tells Spotify "This song is trash."* | *Start the song IMMEDIATELY. No 20s intro skits.* |
| **Playlist Adds** | **Med/High** | *Tells Spotify "People want to keep this."* | *Create your own "JaZeR's Vibe" playlist.* |

## 2. "Radio" & "Autoplay" Triggers

To get on **"Mac Miller Radio"** or **"ProbCause Radio"**, you need **Data Associations**.
*   **The Goal:** Get Spotify to see: "User A listened to Mac Miller, then listened to JaZeR, and didn't skip."
*   **Tactic:** Drive external traffic (Ads/Socials) targeting Mac Miller fans specifically.
*   **Tactic:** Collab with artists who are *already* in that algorithm (e.g., smaller artists who sound like them).

## 3. Metric Benchmarks (Targets)

*   **Save Rate:** Target **>30%** (Excellent). <10% is poor.
*   **Skip Rate:** Target **<35%**. If >50%, your intro is too long or the song doesn't fit the playlist.
*   **Marquee Eligibility:** Need **1,000 streams** in the last 28 days or **5,000 monthly active listeners** (depending on region) to unlock Marquee ads.
*   **Discovery Mode:** Need **25,000 monthly listeners** (Long-term goal).

## 4. Tactical Recommendations

1.  **The "0:00" Rule:** Vocals or melody must start within 0-3 seconds. Do not have a 15-second slow fade-in.
2.  **Structure:** Verse-Chorus within the first 45 seconds. Don't make them wait for the hook.
3.  **Canvas:** A high-quality Canvas can increase share rate by 145%. Use it.
