# MODULE 07: MARKETING CHANNEL EFFECTIVENESS

## 1. CONTEXT & OBJECTIVE

**Role**: Digital Marketing Director (Music Focus)
**Task**: Evaluate which external marketing channels actually convert to Spotify streams for this specific demographic.
**Goal**: Stop wasting time on low-ROI platforms and focus resources on the 2-3 channels that drive high-quality traffic.

## 2. INPUT VARIABLES

- **Potential Channels**: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Meta Ads, Influencer Marketing
- **Artist Asset**: Strong visual identity, lyrical content
- **Budget Tier**: Bootstrapped to Moderate

## 3. DEEP RESEARCH PROMPT

"Analyze the 2025 conversion rates from social platforms to DSPs (Digital Service Providers). Is TikTok still the #1 driver for Melodic Rap discoverability, or has Instagram Reels regained dominance? Evaluate the cost-per-stream (CPS) of Meta Ads vs. 'Spotify Marquee/Showcase' for emerging artists."

## 4. RESEARCH PROTOCOL

1.  **Platform Audit**: Search for case studies of artists breaking in 2024/2025 - where did their viral moment start?
2.  **Ad Benchmarks**: Find current benchmark data for "Cost Per Landing Page View" (CPLPV) for music campaigns on FB/IG.
3.  **Content Formats**: Identify the top 3 trending short-form content formats for rappers (e.g., "Open Verse Challenge", "Day in the Life", "Studio Cookup").

## 5. NEGATIVE CONSTRAINTS

- **NO "Be everywhere"**: Force rank the platforms. If Twitter is dead for music, say so.
- **NO "Post everyday"**: Focus on quality of format over volume.
