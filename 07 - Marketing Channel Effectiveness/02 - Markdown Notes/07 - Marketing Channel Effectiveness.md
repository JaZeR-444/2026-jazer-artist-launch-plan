# 07 - Marketing Channel Effectiveness

## Context
Allocating budget and effort. This prompt compares paid vs. organic channels to find the best ROI for Spotify growth.

## Input Variables
*   **Artist:** `JaZeR`
*   **Budget Tier:** `[e.g., Low ($0-$500), Mid ($1k-$5k), High ($10k+)]`
*   **Current Assets:** `[e.g., Music Video, Vertical Content, Mailing List]`

---

## Deep Research Prompt

**Role:** You are a Growth Marketing Lead for a digital music agency.

**Objective:**
Evaluate which marketing channels yield the best Cost-Per-Stream (CPS) and high-value listener retention for **JaZeR** (Hip-hop/Rap/R&B).

**Research Protocol (Gemini Deep Research):**
1.  **Ad Library Search:** Look for "Meta Ad Library" or "TikTok Creative Center" examples for Hip-hop/R&B artists to see what ads are running.
2.  **Cost Benchmarking:** Search for "Music marketing CPC benchmarks 2025" for Rap/R&B.
3.  **Platform Trends:** Identify which platforms are currently converting best to Spotify (e.g., "TikTok to Spotify conversion rates").

**Instructions:**
1.  **Channel Audit:** Evaluate Meta Ads, TikTok/Shorts, Influencer Seeding, and Direct-to-Fan.
2.  **Attribution Quality:** Analyze traffic quality (Do they stick?).
3.  **Benchmark Costs:** Est. CPC/CPA for this genre.
4.  **Creative Strategy:** What ad creative works best for this demographic?

**Negative Constraints:**
*   **NO "vanity" channels.** If it drives views but not streams, mark it as low value.
*   **NO vague budget advice.** Give % allocations.

**Output Format:**

### 1. Channel ROI Matrix
| Channel | Est. Cost/Effort | Conversion Quality | Scalability | Verdict |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| *TikTok* | *High Effort* | *Variable* | *High* | *Essential* |
| *IG Ads* | *$$$* | *High* | *Linear* | *Scale later* |

### 2. The "Conversion Funnel" Strategy
*   *Step-by-step plan: Content -> Click -> Stream -> Follow.*

### 3. Creative Best Practices
*   *3-5 examples of high-performing ad concepts (e.g., "The Hook," "The Story").*

### 4. Budget/Effort Allocation
*   *Recommended % split of resources based on the input Budget Tier.*
