# 07 - Marketing Channel Effectiveness: JaZeR

## 1. Channel ROI Matrix

| Channel | Est. Cost/Effort | Conversion Quality | Scalability | Verdict |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Instagram Reels** | *High Effort / $0* | *High (Visual connection)* | *Viral Potential* | **ESSENTIAL** |
| **TikTok** | *High Effort / $0* | *Med (Fickle audience)* | *Massive Reach* | **ESSENTIAL** |

| **Playlisting (Paid)** | *$$ (Low/Mid)* | *Low (Passive listeners)* | *Capped* | *Use sparingly for "Social Proof".* |

## 2. The "Conversion Funnel" Strategy

1.  **Top of Funnel (Discovery):**
    *   *Content:* "If you like Mac Miller, you need to hear this..." (Reels/TikTok).
    *   *Goal:* Get them to view the profile.
2.  **Middle of Funnel (Consideration):**
    *   *Content:* Pinned posts showing the *best* song snippet + "The Artist" vibe.
    *   *Goal:* Click the "Link in Bio" (Smart Link).
3.  **Bottom of Funnel (Action):**
    *   *Content:* Spotify Smart Link.
    *   *Goal:* Stream + **FOLLOW** + **SAVE**.

## 3. Creative Best Practices (2025/2026)

*   **"The Hook":** Visually and audibly hook them in 1.5 seconds. Use text overlays: *"POV: You found your new favorite late-night drive song."*
*   **"The Process":** Show the DAW (Logic/Ableton). "How I flipped this sample." Musicians love this, and "Creative Casey" loves it too.
*   **"The Vibe":** Aesthetic shots of Austin/Studio with the song playing. No talking. Just vibe.

## 4. Budget/Effort Allocation

*   **Budget: Low/Bootstrap ($0 - $500)**
    *   **100% Organic Content:** Post 1-2 Reels/TikToks *daily*.
    *   **$0 Ad Spend:** Save money for high-quality cover art or mixing.
*   **Budget: Seed ($500 - $2,000)**
    *   **70% Content Creation:** Better videos, maybe a videographer for a day.
    *   **30% Meta Ads:** Test $5/day ads on Instagram Stories targeting "Mac Miller" interest.
