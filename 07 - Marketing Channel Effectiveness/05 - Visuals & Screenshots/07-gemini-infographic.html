Channel ROI Matrix

We evaluated channels based on three critical factors: Financial Cost, Conversion Quality (listener retention), and Scalability.

Key Insight

TikTok & Reels are the "Essential" engines. They require high effort but offer massive organic reach and high visual connection without direct ad spend. Paid channels should be layered in later.

The strategic path from a stranger scrolling content to a loyal Spotify follower.

Creative Strategy 2025

To convert traffic to streams, the creative must be authentic to the Genre (Hip-hop/R&B). Here are the three winning formats.

🪝

The Hook

Visually and audibly hook them in 1.5 seconds.

"POV: You found your new favorite late-night drive song."
🎹

The Process

Show the DAW (Logic/Ableton). Musicians and fans love seeing how the sample was flipped.

Show the raw session. "How I made this beat."
🌃

The Vibe

Aesthetic shots of the studio or city night life. No talking. Just the track and the mood.

High contrast visuals. Cinematic lighting.

Resource Allocation
