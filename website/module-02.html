🎯 Market Dynamics

The "Austin Factor"

The scene is a "sleeping giant" - smaller than Houston or Atlanta but highly concentrated on quality and live performance. Gatekeepers: KUTX 98.9 ("The Breaks"), KOOP 91.7, Empire Control Room.

Winning Sound

"Neo-Soul Rap" and "Jazz Rap." The market rejects generic "SoundCloud Trap."

Saturation Index

LOW = OPPORTUNITY

Very few executing Mac Miller / ProbCause style at high level

🌊 "Blue Ocean" Opportunity

The "Electronic-Soul" Niche

Most Austin rap is either "Live Band Organic" (Magna Carda) or "Street" (Trap). There is a massive void for the ProbCause/Mac Miller lane.

The Strategy:

Position JaZeR not just as a "Rapper," but as a "Vibe Curator." Use electronic production elements that appeal to the "Lofi Study" and "Chill Electronic" crowds.
