📌 TL;DR

Use "Waterfall" — singles every 4-6 weeks. Don't drop an album until established. After 3 singles, compile into EP.

  • → Release Single 1 (most catchy track first)
  • → Wait 4-6 weeks, release Single 2 as waterfall
  • → Every release needs: Cover + Canvas + 3 Videos + Pre-Save

⚖️ Strategy Comparison

Strategy Pros Cons Verdict
Album Drop Artistic statement Algorithm hates it ❌ AVOID
Waterfall Constant growth High asset demand ✅ BEST
EP Tease Builds a world Slower Phase 2

💧 Waterfall Blueprint

Month 1: Single 1

Most catchy track. Pitch to "Fresh Finds."

Month 2: Single 2

Deeper cut. Waterfall S1 (S2=Track1, S1=Track2).

Month 3: Single 3

High energy or collab. Waterfall S2+S1.

Month 4: EP

3 singles + 2-3 new. EP Release Party at Empire.
