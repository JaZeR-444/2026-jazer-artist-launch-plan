📌 TL;DR

Save Rate >30% and Skip Rate <35% are the keys to Discover Weekly. Start your song IMMEDIATELY (vocals in 0-3 seconds). Use Canvas for +145% share rate.

  • → Ask fans to "Heart" the track in every CTA
  • → No 15-second slow intros — hook them in 3 seconds

The "0:00" Rule

Vocals or melody must start within 0-3 seconds. No 15s fade-ins.

Structure

Verse-Chorus within first 45 seconds. Don't make them wait.

Canvas

High-quality Canvas = +145% share rate. USE IT.
