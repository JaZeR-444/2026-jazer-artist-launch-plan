# 02 - Spotify Market Landscape

## Context
A deep dive into the competitive environment. This prompt uses Deep Research to find real-time market data.

## Input Variables
*   **Target Genre:** `Hip-hop, Rap, R&B`
*   **Target Region:** `USA / Austin, Texas`
*   **Comparable Artists (Peers/Inspirations):** `Mac Miller, ProbCause, Smino, Saba, Anderson .Paak`

---

## Deep Research Prompt

**Role:** You are a Senior Market Analyst at a top music data firm.

**Objective:**
Conduct a live market analysis for the **Melodic/Artistic Hip-hop and R&B** niche in the **USA** and **Austin, Texas**. Identify where the opportunity lies for **JaZeR** by analyzing "Standard Bearers" (Mac Miller, ProbCause) and "Fast Risers" in this specific lane.

**Research Protocol (Gemini Deep Research):**
1.  **Search Current Charts:** Look for the current Spotify Top 50/Viral 50 USA and Austin-specific "Local Pulse" playlists.
2.  **Analyze Growth:** For the "Comparable Artists" and local Austin risers, search for recent growth spikes and correlate them with events (SXSW, ACL, viral hits).
3.  **Identify Trends:** Look for recent discussions on "Alternative Rap" or "Melodic Hip-hop" trends.

**Instructions:**
1.  **Identify Market Leaders & Disruptors:** Find top 5 Established acts in JaZeR's lane and top 5 High-Velocity Risers.
2.  **Reverse-Engineer Growth:** For the Risers, determine *why* they are growing (TikTok, specific ATX venues, curator support).
3.  **Assess Saturation:** Is the "Melodic Rap" space crowded in Austin?
4.  **Find the Blue Ocean:** Identify what is *missing* in the current landscape that **JaZeR** could fulfill.

**Output Format:**

### 1. Competitive Matrix (Live Data)
| Artist Name | Tier | Monthly Listeners | Recent Growth Driver (Evidence) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| *[Name]* | *Established* | *[Number]* | *[Source: e.g., SXSW, Viral Hit]* |

### 2. Market Dynamics Report
*   **The "Austin Factor":** (Current status of the local Hip-hop/R&B scene)
*   **Current Metas:** (What sound/style is winning *right now* in JaZeR's niche?)
*   **Saturation Index:** (Low/Medium/High)

### 3. "Blue Ocean" Opportunity
*   *Specific recommendation on how to position JaZeR to stand out from the Mac Miller/ProbCause comparisons.*
