# MODULE 02: SPOTIFY MARKET LANDSCAPE

## 1. CONTEXT & OBJECTIVE

**Role**: Market Research Analyst (Music Industry Specialist)
**Task**: Map the competitive landscape for Melodic Hip-Hop/R&B, specifically focusing on the pathway from "Emerging" to "Mid-Tier" (100k-500k ML).
**Goal**: Identify the specific sub-niches, successful peer artists, and "Blue Ocean" opportunities where JaZeR can compete and win.

## 2. INPUT VARIABLES

- **Primary Genre**: Melodic Rap / Alternative R&B
- **Vibe Keywords**: Chill, Lyrical, Jazz-influenced, Introspective
- **Geo-Focus**: US Market (with Austin origin story)

## 3. DEEP RESEARCH PROMPT

"Analyze the current Spotify market landscape for 'Melodic Rap' and 'Alternative Hip-Hop' artists who have grown from <5k to >50k listeners in the past 12 months. Identify the specific 'micro-genres' they are tagging (e.g., 'Chill Rap', 'Jazz Rap'). Reverse-engineer the 'Fans Also Like' sections of artists similar to Mac Miller and ProbCause to find the most relevant peer group for JaZeR."

## 4. RESEARCH PROTOCOL

1.  **Micro-Genre Identification**: Use Every Noise at Once and Spotify search auto-complete to find the specific genre tags currently trending for this sound.
2.  **Competitor Cohort Analysis**: Identify 5-7 "North Star" artists (similar style, but 1-2 steps ahead, e.g., 50k-200k ML). Analyze their top 5 cities.
3.  **Trend Spotting**: What visual aesthetics or song structures are common across the top performing tracks in this niche right now?

## 5. NEGATIVE CONSTRAINTS

- **NO superstars**: Do NOT analyze Drake, J. Cole, or Kendrick. They are statistically irrelevant as benchmarks.
- **NO broad genres**: Do not just say "Hip Hop." Be specific (e.g., "Conscious Trap", "Neo-Soul Rap").
- **NO speculation**: Use actual data from "Discovered On" playlists.
