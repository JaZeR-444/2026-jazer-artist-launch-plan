# MODULE 04: PLAYLIST STRATEGY INTELLIGENCE

## 1. CONTEXT & OBJECTIVE

**Role**: Playlist Promotion Strategist
**Task**: Build a targeted database of reachable playlists (Editorial, algorithmic, and third-party) that fit the JaZeR profile.
**Goal**: Move beyond "generic spamming" to precision targeting of curators who actually program this specific sub-genre.

## 2. INPUT VARIABLES

- **Keywords**: Lyrical Flow, Melodic Bars, Chill Hiphop, Fresh Finds
- **Target Moods**: Focus, Relax, Mellow, Upbeat
- **Search Terms**: "Best Independent Rap", "Undiscovered Hip Hop", "Austin Local"

## 3. DEEP RESEARCH PROMPT

"Identify and categorize the top 20 specific Spotify playlists that are high-probability targets for an emerging Melodic Rap artist. Split this list into: 5 'Reach' Editorials (e.g., Mellow Bars), 5 'Algorithmic' targets (playlists that feed Discover Weekly), and 10 'Third-Party' independent curators with contactable info (IG handles or emails). Prioritize lists that have featured 'ProbCause' or similar indie-successful artists."

## 4. RESEARCH PROTOCOL

1.  **Editorial Recon**: Identify which Editorial Playlists have the highest turnover of "New" artists in this genre.
2.  **Curator Hunting**: Search Chartmetric/Linkedin for specific names of Editors managing "Hip Hop" and "R&B" at Spotify North America.
3.  **Indie Verification**: Verify that the Third-Party playlists are legit (not bot farms) by checking their follower/listener graphs if public data exists, or looking for "submission" links.

## 5. NEGATIVE CONSTRAINTS

- **NO Bot Lists**: Exclude any playlist promising "guaranteed streams."
- **NO Major Label Lists**: Do not list "RapCaviar" - it is unattainable at this stage. Focus on "Most Necessary", "Fresh Finds", etc.
- **NO Dead Links**: Verify the playlist has been updated in the last 14 days.
