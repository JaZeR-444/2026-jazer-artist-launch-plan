# 04 - Playlist Strategy Intelligence

## Context
Mapping the ecosystem of playlists. This prompt demands identifying specific curators and entry points, not just general advice.

## Input Variables
*   **Genre:** `Hip-hop, Rap, R&B`
*   **Moods/Keywords:** `[e.g., Chill, Workout, Study, Party, Trap, Soul]`

---

## Deep Research Prompt

**Role:** You are the Head of Streaming Strategy at a major digital distributor.

**Objective:**
Develop a tactical playlisting roadmap for **JaZeR**. Move beyond "submit to editorial" and find the "Gateway" lists.

**Research Protocol (Gemini Deep Research):**
1.  **Identify Playlists:** Search for "Best Hip-hop/R&B Spotify Playlists 2025" and "Independent Rap Curators."
2.  **Analyze Curators:** Look for contact info or submission guidelines for 3rd party curators (e.g., SubmitHub, Groover, IG handles).
3.  **Editorial Recon:** Identify the current editors or specific "Moods" of key Spotify editorial lists (e.g., RapCaviar, Most Necessary, Are & Be, Pollen).

**Instructions:**
1.  **Ecosystem Mapping:** Identify "Gateway" playlists (Indie/User) that feed "Destination" playlists (Editorial).
2.  **Algorithmic Triggers:** specific signals trigger *Discover Weekly* in the Hip-hop/R&B niche?
3.  **Curator Profiling:** Identify key 3rd-party curators.
4.  **Editorial Context:** specific editorial playlists are relevant? What is their "Sonic Signature"?

**Negative Constraints:**
*   **NO dead playlists.** Verify they have been updated recently.
*   **NO generic "just pitch it" advice.** Give specific targets.

**Output Format:**

### 1. Playlist Ecosystem Map
| Playlist Name | Type (Ed/Algo/User) | Est. Followers | Entry Difficulty | Strategic Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| *[Name]* | *Editorial* | *[#]* | *High* | *Credibility* |
| *[Name]* | *User/Indie* | *[#]* | *Low* | *Algo Trigger* |

### 2. "Gateway" Targets (Actionable)
*   *List of 5-10 specific independent curators or user playlists to target first, with submission methods if found.*

### 3. Optimization Checklist
*   **Sonic Fit:** (Does the production match?)
*   **Metadata:** (Mood tags, Genre tags).
*   **Timing:** (Lead times).

### 4. The "Algorithmic Bridge"
*   *Strategy for converting playlist listeners into followers.*
