## 2. "Gateway" Targets (Actionable)

Focus on these independent curators via **SubmitHub** and **Groover** before pitching Editorial.

1.  **Sphere of Hip Hop:** Accepts "Jazzy, Boom-Bap, Lofi, Soulful."
    *   *Pitch Angle:* "For fans of Mac Miller's jazzier side."
2.  **Impulse:** Focuses on "Neo-soul, Alternative R&B."
    *   *Requirement:* No autotune overload. Keep it soulful.
3.  **PlaylistPartner:** Focus on "Chill" and "Study" vibes.
    *   *Strategy:* Good for boosting "Save Rate" with passive listeners.
4.  **Local Austin Lists:** "Austin Music Foundation" or "KUTX" weekly playlists.

## 3. Optimization Checklist

*   [ ] **Sonic Fit:** Does the track have a <15 second intro? (Critical for "Skip Rate").
*   [ ] **Metadata:** Tagged as "Chill," "Melodic," "Urban," "Alternative."
*   [ ] **Timing:** Pitch to Spotify Editorial **at least 2 weeks** before release via Spotify for Artists.
*   [ ] **Visuals:** Canvas is uploaded and matches the "Vibe" (looping animation).

## 4. The "Algorithmic Bridge"

**Goal:** Trigger **Discover Weekly**.
*   **The Math:** You need ~30% Save Rate and low Skip Rate (<35%) in the first week.
*   **The Bridge:**
    1.  Use **Gateway Playlists** (Sphere of Hip Hop) to get the first 1,000 listeners.
    2.  These listeners are "Quality" (they like this genre).
    3.  Their "Saves" tell Spotify: "People who like Mac Miller also like JaZeR."
    4.  Spotify's algorithm then serves JaZeR to *more* Mac Miller fans via Discover Weekly.
