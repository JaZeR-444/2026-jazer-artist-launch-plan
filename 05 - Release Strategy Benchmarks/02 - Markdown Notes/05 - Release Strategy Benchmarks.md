# 05 - Release Strategy Benchmarks

## Context
Benchmarking successful release rollouts for JaZeR's specific lane.

## Input Variables
*   **Artist:** `JaZeR`
*   **Comparable Artists:** `Mac Miller (Faces/Circles era), ProbCause, Saba`
*   **Genre:** `Melodic Hip-hop / R&B`

---

## Deep Research Prompt

**Role:** You are a Product Manager at a label services company.

**Objective:**
Design a "Best-in-Class" release timeline for **JaZeR**, benchmarking the artistic and independent rollouts of artists like **ProbCause** and the posthumous/catalog strategies of **Mac Miller**.

**Research Protocol (Gemini Deep Research):**
1.  **Case Study Search:** Look for "how ProbCause markets his music" or "Mac Miller Circles release strategy."
2.  **Timeline Reconstruction:** Analyze the frequency of singles leading up to EPs/Albums for these artists.
3.  **Asset Audit:** Search for the *visual* assets used (Abstract art, animations, vertical music videos).

**Instructions:**
1.  **Cadence Analysis:** What is the optimal frequency? (e.g., The "slow burn" vs the "waterfall").
2.  **Asset Density:** How much "visual world-building" content supports a release in this niche?
3.  **Pre-Save Mechanics:** What incentives (exclusive art, vinyl pre-orders) work for this audience?
4.  **Post-Release Window:** Tactics to sustain momentum in a "laid-back" genre.

**Output Format:**

### 1. Strategy Benchmark Comparison
| Strategy | Pros | Cons | Best For... |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| *Art-First Rollout* | *Builds brand depth* | *Slower growth* | *JaZeR's niche* |

*   **T-2 Weeks:** (Secondary single, Visual snippet)
*   **Release Day:** (Canvas launch, Austin local event/activation)
*   **Post-Release:** (Remixes, "Making of" content)
