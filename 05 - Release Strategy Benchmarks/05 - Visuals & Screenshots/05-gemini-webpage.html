Executive Summary

To break through in the current algorithmic landscape, JaZeR cannot rely on a traditional "Album Drop" which often results in a single spike followed by silence. Our research benchmarking suggests a "Waterfall Strategy" is the optimal path. This approach turns a 4-song release into a 4-month campaign, keeping the artist in the "Release Radar" loop constantly and building a cohesive visual world.

2. The "Waterfall" Effect Simulated

Why does this work? By releasing Single 2 as a single, but including Single 1 as "Track 2", you trigger a "second life" of streams for the older track. The chart below simulates how streams accumulate over the 4-month period, preventing the "drop-off" seen in traditional releases.

Month 1

Single 1 launches solo. Establish base listeners.

Month 2 & 3

New tracks drag older tracks with them via tracklisting.

Month 4 (EP)

Full collection releases. Cumulative stream peak.

3. The 4-Month Blueprint

A structured timeline to release a new single every 4-6 weeks.

The Vibe

The Banger

"High Energy / Collab"

"The Compilation"

  • ✓3 Singles + 2 New Tracks
  • ✓Release Party (Empire Control Room)
  • ✓Full project push
