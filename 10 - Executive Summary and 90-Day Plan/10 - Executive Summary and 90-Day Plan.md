# MODULE 10: EXECUTIVE SUMMARY & 90-DAY PLAN

## 1. CONTEXT & OBJECTIVE

**Role**: Artist Manager / Chief Strategist
**Task**: Synthesize the findings from Modules 01-09 into a coherent, linear execution plan.
**Goal**: Create a "Monday Morning" action plan that prioritizes the highest-impact activities for the next quarter.

## 2. INPUT VARIABLES

- **Inputs**: Data from Modules 01-09
- **Timeline**: Next 90 Days (Sprint Structure)
- **Constraint**: Limited hours/resources (Focus on 80/20 rule)

## 3. DEEP RESEARCH PROMPT

"Synthesize the entire research sprint into a 'State of the Union' executive summary for JaZeR. Highlights the top 3 'Unfair Advantages' discovered in the market analysis and the top 3 'Critical Weaknesses' to address. Then, construct a week-by-week 90-Day Sprint Plan that focuses heavily on [Insert strongest lever from research, e.g., Playlist Outreach or Short-Form Content]."

## 4. RESEARCH PROTOCOL

1.  **Synthesis**: Look for patterns across the previous modules. Did "Austin" keep coming up as a key advantage? Did "Merch" appear as a missed opportunity?
2.  **Prioritization**: Rank all suggested actions by "Ease of Implementation" vs. "Impact" (ICE Score).
3.  **Roadmapping**: Sequence the actions logically (e.g., don't pitch playlists before fixing the bio).

## 5. NEGATIVE CONSTRAINTS

- **NO New Research**: This module is for synthesis, not new discovery.
- **NO Overwhelm**: Limit the "Daily Tasks" to 3 critical items max.
