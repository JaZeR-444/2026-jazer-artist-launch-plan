# 10 - Executive Summary and 90-Day Plan: JaZeR

## 1. Executive Snapshot

*   **Current Status:** 🔴 **Launch Phase (Red)** - No footprint, blank canvas.
*   **Top Opportunity:** **"The Blue Ocean"** - Filling the void in Austin for a high-quality, melodic, "Mac Miller-esque" electronic-hip-hop artist.
*   **Primary Risk:** **Obscurity.** Getting lost in the noise of "SoundCloud Rappers" by failing to establish a distinct, professional visual and sonic brand.

## 2. Strategic Pillars

1.  **Brand:** "The Vibe Curator." High-art visuals, introspection, creative lifestyle.
2.  **Music:** "Waterfall" Singles. Consistent, high-quality, melodic rap suitable for "Chill" playlists.
3.  **Local:** "Austin Roots." Network with the *live* scene (Empire, KUTX) to build a real physical fanbase.

## 3. The 90-Day "Sprint" Calendar

### **Month 1: Foundation (The "Stealth" Phase)**
*   **Week 1-2: Asset Creation.**
    *   Finalize Single 1 master.
    *   Shoot cover art & Canvas (High aesthetic value).
    *   Set up Spotify for Artists & Social Profiles.
*   **Week 3-4: The Setup.**
    *   Upload Single 1 to DistroKid (4 weeks out).
    *   Pitch to Spotify Editorial (S4A).
    *   Start "Teasing" content on TikTok/Reels ("Building something new in Austin...").

### **Month 2: Launch (The "Hello" Phase)**
*   **Week 5-6: Single 1 Release.**
    *   **Release Day:** Push all socials to Spotify.
    *   Submit to Indie Curators (Sphere of Hip Hop, Impulse).
    *   Check "Release Radar" pickup.
*   **Week 7-8: Momentum.**
    *   Continue content for Single 1.
    *   Upload Single 2 (Waterfall setup).
    *   Attend local shows (Empire/Mohawk) to network with other artists.

### **Month 3: Sustain & Expand (The "Proof" Phase)**
*   **Week 9-10: Single 2 Release.**
    *   Single 1 streams should boost Single 2.
    *   Analyze data: Who is listening? (Cities, Age).
*   **Week 11-12: The First Show.**
    *   Secure a slot on a local showcase or open mic (Reason and Rhyme).
    *   First Merch Drop (Stickers/Tees) for the early supporters.
    *   Review metrics: Are we at >30% Save Rate?

## 4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

1.  **Save Rate:** >30% (Indicates high listener quality).
2.  **Follower Growth:** +50 Followers per month (Organic).
3.  **Monthly Listeners:** Reach 1,000 within 90 days.
4.  **Local Connection:** Make contact with 1 Booker and 3 Local Artists.
