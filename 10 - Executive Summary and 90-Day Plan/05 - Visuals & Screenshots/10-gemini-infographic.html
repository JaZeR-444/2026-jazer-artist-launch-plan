JaZeR Launch Plan

Executive summary and 90-day tactical roadmap to capture the "Blue Ocean" of Austin's melodic electronic-hip-hop scene.

1. Executive Snapshot

The current status is critical but primed for growth. We are starting from zero ("Red") but facing a significant market gap ("Blue Ocean"). The primary risk is fading into obscurity amidst the noise of generic releases.

Current Status

RED PHASE

Launch Phase: No footprint yet.

🌊

Top Opportunity

"The Blue Ocean"

Filling the void in Austin for a high-quality, melodic, "Mac Miller-esque" electronic-hip-hop artist. No direct competitors in the local high-tier scene.

⚠️

Primary Risk: Obscurity

Getting lost in the "SoundCloud Rapper" noise. We combat this through hyper-professional visual branding and distinct sonic quality.

The Synthesis

To succeed, JaZeR must bypass the amateur phase entirely. We treat the debut not as a "try-out" but as a polished product launch. The 90-day sprint focuses on asset banking first, then a waterfall release strategy.

2. Strategic Pillars

Our execution rests on three non-negotiable pillars. These guide every decision, from Instagram captions to mixing choices.

👁️

Brand: The Vibe Curator

High-art visuals, introspection, creative lifestyle. Moving away from "hustle culture" aesthetics to "artistic depth."

💧

Music: Waterfall Singles

Consistent, high-quality, melodic rap suitable for "Chill" playlists. Frequency builds momentum.

🌵

Local: Austin Roots

Network with the LIVE scene (Empire, KUTX). Building a physical fanbase, not just digital ghosts.

3. The 90-Day Sprint

A strict 2-week block schedule to move from zero to live performance readiness.

Month 1: Foundation (The Stealth Phase)

Weeks 1-2: Asset Creation

  • Finalize Single 1 Master
  • Shoot Cover Art & Spotify Canvas (High Aesthetic)
  • Set up Spotify for Artists & Social Handles

Weeks 3-4: The Setup

  • Upload Single 1 to DistroKid (4 weeks out)
  • Pitch to Spotify Editorial (Critical Step)
  • Start "Teasing" content on TikTok/Reels

Month 2: Launch (The "Hello" Phase)

Weeks 5-6: Single 1 Release 🚀

  • Release Day: Push all socials to Spotify
  • Submit to Indie Curators (Sphere of Hip Hop)
  • Monitor "Release Radar" pickup

Weeks 7-8: Momentum

  • Continue content for Single 1
  • Upload Single 2 (Waterfall Setup)
  • Attend local shows (Empire/Mohawk) for networking

Month 3: Sustain (The "Proof" Phase)

Weeks 9-10: Single 2 Release

  • Single 1 streams should boost Single 2 algorithmically
  • Analyze Data: Demographics check (City/Age)

Weeks 11-12: The First Show

  • Secure slot on local showcase/open mic
  • First Merch Drop (Stickers/Tees)
  • Goal Check: >30% Save Rate?
