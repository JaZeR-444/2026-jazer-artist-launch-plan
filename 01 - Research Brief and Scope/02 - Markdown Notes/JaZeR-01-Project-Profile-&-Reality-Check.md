# 01 - Research Brief and Scope: JaZeR

## 1. Project Profile & Reality Check

*   **Artist Tier:** **Launch Phase / Undiscovered**
    *   *Verification:* Comprehensive searches across Spotify, Apple Music, SoundCloud, and Austin music blogs yielded no public footprint for a Hip-hop artist named "JaZeR" in Austin.
    *   *Implication:* The strategy must focus entirely on "Zero to One" growth—building the initial infrastructure, brand identity, and first 1,000 true fans.
*   **Primary Genre/Niche:** **Alternative Melodic Rap / Neo-Soul Hip-Hop**
    *   *Sonic Benchmarks:* Mac Miller (Circles/Swimming era), ProbCause, Anderson .Paak.
*   **Austin Scene Context:**
    *   **Market Fit:** High. Austin has a thriving "Artistic Rap" scene that favors live instrumentation and soulful delivery over generic trap.
    *   **Local Benchmarks:** The path has been paved by artists like **Magna Carda** (Neo-soul/Hip-hop fusion), **Blackillac** (Soul-influenced duo), **Deezie Brown** (Lush, southern soundscapes), and **Blakchyl** (Melodic flows).
    *   **Opportunity:** The "Live Music Capital" branding of Austin specifically rewards hip-hop acts that can translate well to a live band setup or high-energy artistic performance, distinguishing them from "SoundCloud rappers."
*   **The Gap:** **The "Zero to One" Chasm.**
    *   *Current Reality:* 0 Monthly Listeners, 0 Brand Awareness.
    *   *Primary Goal:* Establish a foundational audience (e.g., 100k Monthly Listeners / Regional Tour).
    *   *Bridge:* Requires aggressive content seeding, local networking (getting on bills with the benchmarks listed above), and a digital strategy that targets the specific "Lyrical/Vibe" rap audience.

## 2. Strategic "Deep Dive" Questions (The "Why")

To bridge the gap, the following critical questions must be answered by the subsequent research modules:

1.  **The "Live" Edge:** *How can JaZeR utilize Austin's live venue ecosystem (e.g., Empire Control Room, Mohawk, Parish) to bypass the crowded digital market?* (Focus for Module 08)
2.  **Visual Identity:** *What visual aesthetics (album art, socials) are currently resonating with fans of Mac Miller/ProbCause, and how can JaZeR adopt this "Artistic" signaling to stand out from generic releases?* (Focus for Module 03/05)
3.  **Algorithmic Entry Point:** *Since JaZeR has no data, what specific "start-up" playlists (e.g., "Mellow Bars," "Jazz Rap," or local "Austin Hip-Hop" lists) act as the most accessible entry points for this sub-genre?* (Focus for Module 04/06)
4.  **Collaborative Network:** *Who are the specific local producers (e.g., those working with Magna Carda or JaRon Marshall) that JaZeR needs to network with to gain immediate sonic credibility?* (Focus for Module 02)
