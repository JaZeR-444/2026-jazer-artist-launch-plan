4. Strategic Execution

How to capture this audience? The strategy shifts from "selling a track" to "inviting them into a creative process."

1

Visual Aesthetic

Cover art must look like high art. Abstract, painted, or styled photography. Reference Faces or Circles.

2

Content Angle

Don't just post the song. Post "The Process." Late night studio vibes, beat breakdowns, and the creative struggle.

3

Merch Strategy

Create a clothing brand, not band merch. High-quality heavyweight tees with minimalist design.
