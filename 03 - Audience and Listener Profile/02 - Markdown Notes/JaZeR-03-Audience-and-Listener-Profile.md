## 4. Strategic Implication

*   **Visuals Matter:** "Casey" is a creative. Cover art cannot be generic. It must look like *art*—abstract, painted, or highly stylized photography (Mac Miller's *Faces* or *Circles* aesthetic).
*   **Content Angle:** Don't just post "New Music Out Now." Post "The process of making this beat" or "Late night studio vibes." Show the *creative lifestyle* that Casey aspires to.
*   **Merch:** No cheap gildan tees. Think "Museum Gift Shop" quality. Tote bags, high-quality prints, or "dad hats" work better than loud logos.
