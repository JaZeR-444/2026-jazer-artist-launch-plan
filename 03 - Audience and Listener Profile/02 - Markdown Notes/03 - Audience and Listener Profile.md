# 03 - Audience and Listener Profile

## Context
Understanding the human behind the stream for JaZeR.

## Input Variables
*   **Artist/Project:** `JaZeR`
*   **Core Influences:** `Mac Miller, ProbCause`
*   **Key Vibes/Aesthetics:** `Artistic, Melodic, Chill, Eclectic, Austin-Modern`

---

## Deep Research Prompt

**Role:** You are a Consumer Insights Director specializing in Youth Culture.

**Objective:**
Construct a detailed persona of the core listener for **JaZeR**. Focus on fans of melodic, eclectic Hip-hop and R&B who value artistic depth (the "Mac Miller" demographic).

**Research Protocol (Gemini Deep Research):**
1.  **Social Listening:** Search for Reddit threads (r/MacMiller, r/hiphopheads), Discord servers, or Austin-based music communities.
2.  **Comment Analysis:** Look for common themes in comments for artists like Mac Miller, ProbCause, and Smino.
3.  **Cross-Reference:** Connect the music to other interests (Streetwear, Skating, Modern Art, Texas "Keep Austin Weird" culture).

**Instructions:**
1.  **Demographics & Geography:** Identify key age brackets (e.g., Gen Z / Millennials) and "Super Markets" (Austin, Houston, LA, NY, Denver).
2.  **Psychographics & Identity:**
    *   **Values:** Authenticity, Mental Health awareness, Chill vibes, Creative independence.
    *   **Aesthetics:** Modern streetwear, vintage/thrift, minimalist design.
3.  **Digital Habitat:** Where do they live online? (Discord, Reddit, TikTok).
4.  **Consumption Habits:** When/How do they listen? (Late-night drives, Studying, Social gatherings).

**Output Format:**

### 1. The "Superfan" Persona
*   **Name:** (e.g., "Creative Casey")
*   **Mantra:** (Quote summarizing their worldview)
*   **Top 5 Artists:** (Who else is in their rotation?)

### 2. Geographic Heatmap (Data-Backed)
*   *Top 5 Countries & Top 10 Cities with rationale (Focusing on the US/Texas/Global hubs).*

### 3. Digital & Lifestyle Map
*   **Non-Music Brands:** (Clothing, Tech, Lifestyle).
*   **Content Preferences:** (Aesthetic visuals, BTS, creative process).

### 4. Strategic Implication
*   *How to tailor JaZeR's content to fit this lifestyle.*
