# MODULE 03: AUDIENCE & LISTENER PROFILE

## 1. CONTEXT & OBJECTIVE

**Role**: Consumer Insights Specialist
**Task**: Build a high-fidelity persona of the "Superfan" for JaZeR's style of music.
**Goal**: Understand exactly who is listening to this genre, where they hang out online, and what drives them to convert from "Listener" to "Follower."

## 2. INPUT VARIABLES

- **Music Style**: Melodic, Lyrical, Chill, Vibe-focused
- **Likely Demographics**: Gen Z / Young Millennial (18-34)
- **Cultural Markers**: Festival culture, stoner rap, lo-fi study beats, emotional intelligence

## 3. DEEP RESEARCH PROMPT

"Develop a detailed psychographic and demographic listener profile for fans of 'Mac Miller', 'ProbCause', and 'Kota the Friend'. identifying their cross-platform behaviors. What subreddits do they frequent? What fashion brands overlap with this music taste? What are their peak listening times and contexts (e.g., Studying, Smoking, Driving)?"

## 4. RESEARCH PROTOCOL

1.  **Audience Overlap Search**: Look for "Fans of [Artist] also listen to..." threads on Reddit (r/hiphopheads, r/macmiller).
2.  **Contextual Analysis**: Search Spotify for user-generated playlists containing these artists. What are the playlist titles? (e.g., "Late Night Drives", "Sunday Morning").
3.  **Brand Affinity**: Identify 3 non-music brands that frequently partner with artists in this lane.

## 5. NEGATIVE CONSTRAINTS

- **NO demographic generalizations**: "Everyone likes music" is not an answer.
- **NO vague contexts**: "Chilling" is too broad. Use "Gaming session background" or "Post-party comedown."
