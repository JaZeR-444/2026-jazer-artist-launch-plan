# 08 - Touring and Spotify Demand Signals: JaZeR

## 1. Top Markets & Venue Targets

**Home Base: Austin, TX**
*   **Strategy:** You must conquer home first.
*   **Target Venues:**
    *   **Empire Control Room:** *The* spot for Hip-Hop/Electronic. (Contact: `BookingAssistant@heardent.com`)
    *   **Flamingo Cantina:** Good for smaller, energetic shows.
    *   **Far Out Lounge:** Great for "Chill/Vibe" outdoor shows.

**Expansion Targets (The "Texas Triangle")**
*   **Houston, TX:** (White Oak Music Hall - Upstairs). Massive market, just 2.5 hours away.
*   **San Antonio, TX:** (Paper Tiger). Very accessible.
*   **Dallas, TX:** (Deep Ellum Art Co).

## 2. Routing Map (The "Weekend Warrior")

For a new artist, don't tour for weeks. Do **"Weekend Runs"**.

*   **Friday:** San Antonio (Paper Tiger)
*   **Saturday:** Austin (Empire Control Room - Home Show)
*   **Sunday:** Houston (White Oak)

## 3. "Soft Hold" Strategy

*   **Don't book a tour yet.** You have 0 listeners.
*   **The "Showcase" Strategy:** Instead of headlining, submit to **Local Showcases**.
    *   *Action:* Look for "Reason and Rhyme" nights or openers for touring acts similar to you.
    *   *Action:* Monitor **Mohawk** and **Empire** calendars. When a "Melodic Rap" artist is announced, email the booker *immediately* to ask for the opening slot. Send your EPK and Spotify links.

## 4. Local Activation Tactics (ATX)

*   **"Ticket for a Follow":** If you get a door deal, offer cheap entry if they show they follow you on Spotify at the door.
*   **The "Local Legend":** Collaborate with Austin visual artists for your cover art. They will bring *their* crowd to the show.
