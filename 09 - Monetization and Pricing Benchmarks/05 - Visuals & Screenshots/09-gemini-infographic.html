The Value Ladder

From casual listener to superfan investor.

Streaming is not the revenue driver; it is the marketing channel. The goal is to migrate users from the low-value "Entry" tier (Spotify streams) up to "Core" and "Premium" tiers where profit margins are sustainable. We prioritize Core items like T-shirts for volume and Premium items like Hoodies for margin.

The "First 100" Drop Strategy

A scarcity-driven launch model to build the "Founders Club."

01

Design & Source

Create "Minimalist Streetwear." Avoid giant logos. Source 25-50 units of high-quality blanks. Goal is Cool, not Billboard.

02

The "Founders" Hook

Market strictly to top listeners via DM or Email. "Founders Club" exclusive access. Creates 25 walking billboards.

03

Direct Sales

Use Shopify Starter or BigCartel. Keep it lean. Goal is breakeven + buzz. Do not overstock inventory.

04

Scale & Integrate

Reinvest profits into "Always On" core collection. List on Merchbar to sync with Spotify profile.
