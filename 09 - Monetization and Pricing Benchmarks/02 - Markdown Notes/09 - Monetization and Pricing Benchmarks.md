## Deep Research Prompt

**Role:** You are a Business Manager and CFO for creative talent.

**Objective:**
Build a diversified revenue model for **JaZeR** that leverages the Spotify audience to drive high-margin sales.

**Research Protocol (Gemini Deep Research):**
1.  **Merch Trends:** Search for "Best selling rapper merch items 2025" and "Streetwear trends 2025."
2.  **Pricing Benchmarks:** Look at merch stores of comparable Hip-hop/R&B artists to see price points for Tees, Hoodies, Vinyl.
3.  **Platform rates:** Check fees for Merchbar, Shopify, Patreon.

**Instructions:**
1.  **The "Value Ladder":** Define products from Free (Stream) to High Ticket (VIP).
2.  **Merch Strategy:** What items sell best for this demographic? (e.g., Hoodies vs Tees).
3.  **Pricing Power:** Benchmark pricing against comparable artists.
4.  **Conversion Pathways:** Bridge the gap between Spotify and Point of Sale.

**Negative Constraints:**
*   **NO generic items.** (Don't just say "T-shirts"). Suggest design directions or specific garment types (e.g., "Heavyweight Boxy Tee").
*   **NO ignoring margins.** Focus on profit.
