# 09 - Monetization and Pricing Benchmarks: JaZeR

## 1. Revenue Model Canvas

| Product Tier | Item | Price Point ($) | Est. Margin | Why? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Entry (Traffic)** | **Spotify Stream** | *$0.003* | *~0%* | *Marketing Channel. Not income.* |
| **Core (Profit)** | **T-Shirt** | *$25 - $30* | *40-50%* | *The standard supporter badge.* |
| **Premium (Profit)** | **Hoodie** | *$45 - $60* | *40%* | *High value item for superfans.* |
| **VIP (High Ticket)** | **Limited Vinyl** | *$30 - $40* | *Var* | *Collectors item (Future goal).* |

## 2. Merch "Drop" Strategy

*   **Design:** **Minimalist Streetwear.** Do NOT just put "JaZeR" in big letters.
    *   *Concept:* Abstract art, "Vibe" quotes, small embroidered logos. People want to wear *cool clothes*, not just a billboard for a new artist.
*   **The "First 100" Drop:**
    *   Order a small batch (25-50 units).
    *   Market it as "Founders Club."
    *   Sell it directly via DM or a simple BigCartel/Shopify site.
    *   *Goal:* Breakeven on the cost + create 25 walking billboards in Austin.

## 3. Pricing Benchmarks (2025/2026)

*   **T-Shirts:** Indie standard is **$25**. If it's "Heavyweight" or "Vintage Wash," you can charge **$30-$35**.
*   **Hoodies:** Standard **$45**. High quality **$60**.
*   **Stickers:** **Free** (or $5 for a pack). Give these away at shows to get your name on laptops and water bottles.

## 4. Spotify Integration

*   **Merchbar:** Once you have physical stock, list it on Merchbar to have it appear on your Spotify profile.
*   **Artist Pick:** Use "Artist Pick" to highlight your Merch store if you are running a campaign, or your next Show.
